Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 3, 2026 Â· Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Welcome to Ryuuda, a multi-purpose Discord bot designed to enhance your server experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with Ryuuda. Your privacy is important to us, and we're committed to transparency about our data practices.

Quick Summary: Ryuuda only collects data necessary for functionality. We don't sell your data, and we minimize what we store. Most data is deleted within 30 days unless required for security or legal purposes.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, Ryuuda collects the following categories of information:

User Information

Discord User ID, username, and discriminator to identify users and provide personalized features.

Server Information

Server ID, channel IDs, role information, and basic server settings for server-specific functionality.

Interaction Data

Command usage, timestamps, and contextual information needed to process your requests.

Logs

Error reports and performance metrics to maintain service quality and troubleshoot issues.

What We Don't Collect: We do not collect personal messages outside of bot commands, private conversations, or any sensitive personal information beyond what Discord provides through their API.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve Ryuuda's services:

  • Service Delivery: Processing commands, managing server features, and maintaining bot functionality
  • Improvement & Development: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Security & Safety: Preventing abuse, enforcing our Terms of Service, and protecting our services
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and Discord's Developer Terms
  • Communication: Sending important updates about the bot's status or policy changes (when necessary)

3. Data Storage & Security

Storage Duration

We follow data minimization principles:

  • Command logs and interaction data: Typically deleted within 30 days
  • Server configuration data: Retained as long as the bot remains in your server
  • Error logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Backup data: Encrypted and retained for up to 60 days

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Secure infrastructure following best practices

While we take reasonable precautions, no online service can guarantee 100% security. We encourage users to protect their Discord accounts with strong passwords and two-factor authentication.

4. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data stored by Ryuuda.

Right to Deletion

Request removal of your data, subject to legal requirements.

Right to Correction

Request updates to inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Opt-Out

Remove Ryuuda from your server at any time to stop data collection.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information in Section 7. We will respond within 30 days.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental request
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, users, or the public from harm
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

6. International Data Transfers

Ryuuda operates globally. Your data may be processed in countries outside your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place and comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for EU/EEA users.

7. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Discord Support: Join our support server at discord.gg/kTgDYZra29
  • Email: ryuuda.privacy@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 7 business days

For EU/UK Users: Ryuuda acts as a data processor for Discord's data controller. For GDPR/UK GDPR inquiries, you may contact both us and Discord. Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest and contractual necessity.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be:

  • Announced in our Discord server
  • Noted with an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page
  • Communicated through the bot's status message for 7 days following changes

Continued use of Ryuuda after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Children's Privacy

Ryuuda is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in some regions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10. Discord's Role

Ryuuda operates within Discord's platform. Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service also apply to your use of their platform. We encourage you to review Discord's policies to understand their data practices.

Thank you for trusting Ryuuda. We're committed to protecting your privacy while providing an excellent Discord experience. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
